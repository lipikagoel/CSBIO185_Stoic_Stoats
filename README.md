# C&S BIO 185 Stoic Stoats Final Project
## Analyzing SNP-linked gene expression across fibrotic diseases
Final project code for Computational & Systems Biology 185: Thesis Research Opportunities in Computational and Systems Biology.

**Contributors:** Abby Bradley, Lipika Goel, Zofia Lok, Darcy Niu

### Project Description:
Dupuytren disease (DD) is a type of fibrosis that affects the palm of the hand and causes fingers to contract. Through looking at SNPs of cases and controls, Ophoff et al. found 26 loci (17 new, 9 validated from previous study) that are associated with DD. The loci identified in this study could be important for other types of fibrotic disease.
**_Among the genes referenced in this paper to be potentially associated with the identified DD-correlated SNPs, is there a pattern of differential expression of these genes in idiopathic pulmonary fibrosis (IPF), a similar tissue-specific fibrotic disease?_**
